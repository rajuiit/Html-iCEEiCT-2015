The 2nd International Conference on Electrical Engineering and Information Communication Technology (ICEEICT 2015) aims to bring together leading academic scientists, researchers and scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results about all aspects of Electrical, Electronics, Communication Engineering and IT, and discuss the practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted. The conference is jointly organized by Institute of Information Technology of Jahangirnagar University, Savar, Dhaka-1342 and Dept. of Electrical Electronics & Communication Engineering of Military Institute of Science and Technology, Mirpur, Dhaka. Prospective authors are invited to submit original technical papers for presentation at the conference and publication in the conference proceedings. All submitted papers will be peer reviewed. The paper of the last conference has been included in IEEE Xplore Digital Library and this time all accepted, registered and presented papers will be included in IEEE Xplore Digital Library.
