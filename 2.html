Brief CV of Professor Ranjan Gangopadhyay

Prof. R.Gagopadhyay, PhD was former Professor and Head in the Department of Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kharagpur. He was also an Emeritus Professor in the G.S.Sanyal School of Telecommunication, I.I.T Kharagpur. In 1980, he joined The LNM Institute of Information Technology (a Deemed University), Jaipur, Rajasthan, and has been serving the same Institute as an Emeritus Professor till now. Prof.Gangopadhyay was the Co-ordinator, Photonics Mission Project, in IIT-Kharagpur He was Visiting Professor in the University of Parma, Italy, Chonbuk National University, Korea; Scuola Superiore Sant”Anna, Pisa, Italy. He was recipient of European Commission Senior Post-Doctoral Fellowship, INSA-Royal Society Fellowship (x 2); JSPS (Japan) Senior Fellowship; CIDA (Canada) Fellowship. He was a Scientific Advisory Committee Member in Scuola Sant “Anna, Pisa, Italy for three consecutive terms He serves as a Referee for Peer Review Evaluation, European Commission till 2013. Prof Gangopadhya was also the Chief Investigator of Bilateral Projects: European Commission, Indo-Italy, Indo-Japan, Indo-UK; as well as the Chief Investigator of Projects funded by National Agencies: DST, DOE, ISRO, MHRD and DiCT. He has published more than 100 Research papers in Journals and Conferences; supervised 8 PhDs; and has to his credit over 40 years of experience in Teaching and Research. He has contributed significantly in the research areas such as Nonlinear Photonics, Light-wave Networks and Systems and Broadband Communication.


Evolutionary Scenario of DWDM Technology

Abstract: The data communication service growth at the present time in terms of data rate efficiency and versatility has been phenomenal in the Photonics Communication arena both in terrestrial, local access and enterprise networks. The talk will provide a glimpse of the evolutionary scenario of DWDM Technology along with the current achievements and future trends.
